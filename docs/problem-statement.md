# Problem Statement

Digital banking platforms provide access to a large number of services, but customers often need to discover those services on their own.

As a result:

- Useful features remain underutilized,
- Recommendations are generic,
- Customer engagement declines over time.

There is an opportunity to make digital banking more contextual by understanding customer activity and presenting relevant guidance at appropriate moments.

The objective of SBI Compass is to improve digital engagement through event-driven recommendations informed by customer history and stated goals.
